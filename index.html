Cross, Drugs & Food

Cross, Drugs & Food is the result of the collective effort, creativity and dedication of the eight students that are part of the practical seminar led by Mrs.(i'm sry idk which one) Biendl.
Cross, Drugs & Food is a play about murder, drugs and seemingly crazy students in an even crazier school headed by an yet even crazier headmistress. You can observe all that while having a 3-menu dinner and also being able to tell us who's the murderer in the end.
